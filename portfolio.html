Digital Artwork

In 2022 I received my first iPad and Apple Pencil and in that time I started learning
how to enhance my illustrations. Since then I have made over 100 pieces of digital artwork.
I use Procreate and Vectornator for my art. I have created logos, portraits, and other art.
I had an Etsy at one point and was successful in selling some of my designs as well as
created new ones for the buyer. Buyers can send me a picture reference of what they
would like drawn out, and I would create cartoonized portaits.

Beginning a career in digital art and web design can be an exciting and rewarding experience.
Digital art and web design are two of the most popular and in-demand fields in the world today,
and they offer a wide range of opportunities for creative expression and professional growth.

It is important to stay up to date on the latest trends and technologies in digital art and web design.
This will help you stay competitive in the field and ensure that you are creating work that is relevant
and up to date. Additionally, it is important to stay connected with other professionals in the field,
as this can help you stay informed about new opportunities and stay ahead of the competition.

Overall, beginning a career in digital art and web design can be an exciting and rewarding experience.
With the right tools, knowledge, and dedication I can create beautiful and engaging work that will help me
stand out in the field.
